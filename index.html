About the Artist

Robert Rosina is an Australian-born film composer. He studied film at Macquarie University (Sydney), majoring in Screen Practice and Production (covering film analysis and sound design) with a minor in Music (including screen soundtracks and vocal performance). He is a highly skilled guitarist, classically trained vocalist, and multi-instrumentalist with two decades of experience composing, arranging, and performing across diverse genres. His soundtracks blend primal vocals and piercing guitar textures with orchestral harmony and modern sound design to create a distinctive cinematic voice.

Before his music career, Robert earned a law degree and worked in corporate law, demonstrating reliability, meeting tight deadlines, and maintaining exceptional attention to detail. He has also studied drama and film acting at several institutions, including the Actor's Pulse, to deepen his understanding of cinematic storytelling.
