About the Artist

Robert Rosina is an Australian-born film composer who crafts distinctive, evocative scores that blend cinematic guitar textures, primal vocals, orchestral harmony, and modern sound design.

He studied film at Macquarie University (Sydney), majoring in Screen Practice and Production—including film analysis, sound design, and screen storytelling—and minored in Music, with training in screen soundtracks and vocal performance. A highly skilled guitarist, classically trained vocalist, and multi-instrumentalist, he brings two decades of experience composing, arranging, and performing across diverse genres.

Robert’s approach to scoring is rooted in narrative purpose, collaborating with directors, producers, and game developers to deliver emotionally resonant, production-ready music tailored to each project’s vision. His work is informed by deep study of acting and cinematic storytelling at institutions including the Actor's Pulse, ensuring a composer who understands performance, subtext, and scene dynamics.

Before his music career, Robert earned a law degree and worked in corporate law—an experience that honed his precision, accountability, and discipline in meeting tight deadlines. As a business owner, he is also committed to clear communication, client service, and reliable delivery.
