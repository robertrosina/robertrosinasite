About the Artist

Robert Rosina is a composer for film, television, and games, blending hybrid orchestral scoring with cinematic guitar and vocals. With a background in film and music studies from Macquarie University, he composes scores shaped by the emotional needs of the story.

Rosina delivers the dramatic, emotionally structured scores expected in modern Hollywood, while also carving out a distinct voice that draws from progressive instrumental guitar—a cinematic genre criminally underutilised by the industry due to the scarcity of guitar virtuoso composers.

His 2025 concept album Chaos Theory embodies this approach, exploring his signature sound across a narrative arc of collapse and reawakening. As with all his projects, he recorded his own palette of guitars, vocals, percussion, and orchestral instruments to ensure a handcrafted musical identity.

Rosina’s first career was in corporate law, fostering discipline, collaboration, and deadline precision. Today, he brings that same professionalism to narrative scoring, working with directors to craft music that gives modern film a bold new voice.
